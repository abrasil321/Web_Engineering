University History

Lions University is a for-profit university
headquartered in Idaho. Founded in 1976, the school
confers certificates and degrees in over 100 certificate
programs and degree programs at the certificate, associate,
bachelor's, master's, and doctoral degree levels. It is
regionally accredited by the Higher Learning Commission and
has an open enrollment admission policy, accepting all
applicants with a high-school diploma, GED, or its equivalent
as sufficient for admission. In 2017, the school's parent
company, LDS Education, was acquired by Thomas Global Management,
an American private equity firm.
